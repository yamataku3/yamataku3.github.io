About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student at Lifestyle Computing Lab (PI: Yuta Sugiura), Keio University, Japan.

I am also a Research Assistant in the Digital Human Research Team, AIRC, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST), advised by Dr. Suguru Kanoga.

Previously, I was a visiting PhD student at the HCI Engineering Group at MIT CSAIL (advised by Prof. Stefanie Mueller and Dr. Jiaji Li) and a visiting student in the School of Computer Science at the University of Sydney (advised by Prof. Anusha Withana).
